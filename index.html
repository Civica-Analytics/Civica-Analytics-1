Age and Education Demographics

Hover over the bubbles to take an in-depth look at the percentage breakdown of Republican voters and their numbers in the millions. Additionally, you can take a look at the percentage breakdown for the different possible education levels people in the party have: "High school diploma or less", "Some college or associate's degree", "Bachelor's degree", and "Advanced degree". You can also view the Republican voter breakdown of the different age demographics: "Millenials", "Gen X", "Boomer", and "Silent/Greatest" Generations.
